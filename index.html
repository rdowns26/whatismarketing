what is marketing is a crowdsourced project to answer your marketing questions. We provide information, actionable steps, and connections for learning more.

Welcome, we're glad you joined us!

Take a moment and ask yourself "what is marketing?" If you're stuck or feel like your answer is missing something, that's ok. People joke that if you ask 100 experts the same question, you'll get 100 different answers. That's why we started this project.

Marketing can take several shapes and forms. And each relies on different skills and experiences. A career in marketing won't be a straight path with steps laid in front of you.

But that's why we love it.

Start exploring.

A mix of enthusiastic students, professors, mentors, and industry experts who are passionate about helping you decode marketing. Special thanks to the McCombs Marketing Department, Career Services, and officers of the Texas American Marketing Association.
